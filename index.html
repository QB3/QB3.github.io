Quentin BERTRAND - Home

 

I am a post doctoral researcher at Mila working with Gauthier Gidel and Simon Lacoste-Julien.
I work on the links between game theory and bilevel optimization.

Prior to this position, I did my Ph. D. at Inria Paris-Saclay (in the Parietal Team) under the supervision of Joseph Salmon and Alexandre Gramfort.
I worked on the optimization and statistical aspects of high dimensional sparse linear regression applied to brain signals reconstruction.
In particular, I developped a python package for fast computation and automatic hyperparameter selection of sparse linear models.
