Quentin BERTRAND - Home

 

I am a 2nd year PhD student in machine learning at Inria Paris-Saclay (in the Parietal Team), under the supervision of Joseph Salmon and Alexandre Gramfort.
I work on the optimization and statistical aspects of high dimensional sparse linear regression applied to brain signals reconstruction. I have a keen interest in the Python programming language: I am a contributor to MNE.
I also develop a python package for automatic hyperparameter selection of sparse linear models.
