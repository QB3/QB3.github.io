Quentin BERTRAND - Home

 

I am a post doctoral researcher at Mila working with Gauthier Gidel and Simon Lacoste-Julien. Prior to this position, I did my Ph. D. at Inria Paris-Saclay (in the Parietal Team) under the supervision of Joseph Salmon and Alexandre Gramfort.

I work on the optimization and statistical aspects of high dimensional sparse linear regression applied to brain signals reconstruction. I have a keen interest in coordinate descent algorithms, hyperparameter selection, and the Python programming language.
I also develop a python packages for fast computation and automatic hyperparameter selection of sparse linear models.
